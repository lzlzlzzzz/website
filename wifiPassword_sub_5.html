How to login to school desktops quickly

Do it in 2 steps.

Sometimes when you login to the school desktops, they can be loading like this for a long time. When this happens, it is best to patiently wait for three or four minutes. But if you have something urgent, here is what you could do (it usually works well):

1. Press "Ctrl" + "Alt" + "Delete"

2. Select "Cancel"

Done!
