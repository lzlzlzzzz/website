How to print a file(s) that is in a zip file uploaded by your teacher

Do it in 6 steps.

Sometimes your teacher puts a zip file on the Google Classroom. Here is what to do if you wish to print a file(s) in a zip file:

1. Open the zip attachment, click on the three dots ("More actions") at the top right corner, then select "Open in new window"

2. Click on the Download icon at the top right corner to download the zip file

3. Unzip the zip file

4. Upload the file(s) that you wish to print to your Google Drive

OR: Email the file(s) to yourself

5. Open the file(s), click on the printer icon at the top right corner and release the printing job(s)

6. Don't forget to LOGOUT once you have finished!

Done!
