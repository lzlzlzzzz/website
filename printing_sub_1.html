How to print without an ID card

Do it in 7 steps.

Swiping your student ID card has been a quick and easy way to log into the school printer. However, you can still print if you have not yet been issued an ID card, or if you forgot to bring it. Here is how to do it:

1. Release your printing job

2. Enter your username (i.e. your student ID number) to the school printer

3. Enter your password (i.e. your school Gmail account password)

4. Hit "Log in"

5. Select the file that you want to print

6. Hit "Print"

7. Don't forget to LOGOUT once you have finished!

Done!
