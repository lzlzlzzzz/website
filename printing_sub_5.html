How to scan

Do it in 10 steps.

Scan is a very useful function of our school printer. Here are the instructions on how to scan:

1. Swipe your ID card or login via your username and password

2. Select “Use Device Function”

3. Select “Scanner” at the top of the screen

4. Select “Scan to me”

5. Lay your document on the top left corner of the glass

6. Change Scan Settings (colours, resolution, scan size, etc) if desired

7. Hit “Start”

8. Lay the next document on the glass within the next 60 seconds, then hit “Start” again, repeat as needed

9. Once you have done scanning all your documents, hit the hush ("#") key. A file will be sent to your Gmail

10. Don’t forget to LOGOUT once you have finished!

Done!
