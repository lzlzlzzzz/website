How to connect to the school WiFi on an Android device

Do it in 8 steps.

Connecting to the school WiFi network on Android devices is slightly more complicated than that on Apple devices. Here are the instructions on how to do it:

1. Go to WiFi settings and select our school Wifi - "WGHS_BYOD"

2. Enter your school Gmail account password to "Password"

3. Select “PEAP” as the "EAP method"

4. Select "none" as the 2nd authentication

5. Select "(Unspecified)" as the "CA certificate"

6. Enter your student ID number as the "Identity"

7. You may leave the "Anonymous identity" blank. But if you want to fill it in, it is also your student ID number

8. Click on "Connect" once you have finished

Done!
