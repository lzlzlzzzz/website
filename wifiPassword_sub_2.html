How to change a password

Do it in 8 steps.

Your school password expires every six months (you will receive an email notification 10 days before it expires). When this happens, you will need to use one of our school desktops or laptops to change your password. Here are the instructions:

1. Log into a school desktop/laptop

2. Once you have logged in, press "Ctrl" + "Alt" + "Delete"

3. Select â€œChange a password"

4. Put in your old password

5. Put in your new password (it may be the same as your old one)

6. Confirm your new password

7. Click on the arrow next to the confirmed new password, or press the "Enter" key

Done!
