How choose a suitable password

Do it in 5 steps.

There are five requirements for your school password. By following the steps you will be able to choose a suitable and valid password. The school password:

1. Must be at least 8 characters long

2. Must contain at least ONE upper-case letter

3. Must contain at least ONE lower-case letter

4. Must contain at least ONE number or symbol

5. Can NOT include your name

Done!
