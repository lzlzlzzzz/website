About Westlake

Westlake Girls High School is a state girls secondary school, located to the west of Lake Pupuke in Takapuna, Auckland, New Zealand. The school was established in 1958 as a coeducational school, changing to girls only in 1962 when Westlake Boys High School opened. Westlake Girls has a roll of approximately 2297 girls from Years 9 to 13 (ages 12 to 18), making one of the largest single-sex schools in New Zealand.
