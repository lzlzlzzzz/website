How to reconnect to the school WiFi once you have changed your password

Do it in 4 steps.

Once you have changed your password, you will need to FORGET the school WiFi on your devices and then REJOIN. Otherwise, each time you try to connect to the school WiFi, you are entering the WRONG password, and your account may be locked eventually! (If this happens, please see our librarians.) Here are the reconnection instructions:

1. Go to WiFi settings and select our school Wifi - "WGHS_BYOD"

2. Click on "Forget network"

3. Go to WiFi settings and select our school Wifi again

4. Fill in all the details (especially your new password) and then "Connect"

Done!
